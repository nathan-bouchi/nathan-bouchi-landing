Our project

We are a start up which has the project of developing an artificial intelligence specific to companies, and which adapts to their personal needs. The idea is to save companies time on rather simple and repetitive tasks which an artificial intelligence could take care of.

Our founder

Nathan Bouchi

"Artificial intelligence is an integral part of the future of business, it can allow companies to improve in all these damages and support it if it is well developed to adapt to its needs. This is why I created the Codex project, an AI which learns with the decisions of the company and offers it the best choices for its development and its expansion. "
